About Me

However, this is not my first endeavour into Computing. I also studied A level Computer Science at Trinity Sixth Form, alongside Mathematics, Philosophy and Ethics, and History.

Two of these instruments I play in bands, which demonstrates my ability to work with others in a team, under stressful, and demanding situations.

Thank you for taking the time to look at my website! I look forward to working with you.
